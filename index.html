01 — About

Design that thinks
before it speaks

I'm Shivang Mehra, a UI/UX designer with 5+ years of experience building digital products for agencies, startups, and freelance clients — from websites and mobile apps to full web applications.

My design philosophy is simple: aesthetics and function are not a tradeoff. I craft design that is functional at the core — things that don't just look better but also work better.

Currently working full-time at Dstudio Agency while pursuing a BBA in Digital Business & Entrepreneurship at IIM Bangalore.

Design principles

Function first, alwaysGreat design solves a real problem. Beauty is the outcome, not the goal.

Clarity over clevernessIf a user has to think, something failed.

Details are the designThe margin, the motion, the micro-interaction — that's where trust is built.
